Privacy Policy for Flash Shot Freshness Guard

Effective Date: January 2025

Last Updated: January 2025

Privacy First: Flash Shot Freshness Guard is designed with privacy as a core principle. All your data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Food Item Data: Information about food items you add, including names, expiration dates, and categories
  • App Settings: Your preferences for notifications, language, and voice settings
  • Photos: Images you take of food items for scanning purposes

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Basic device information for app functionality
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app features (stored locally only)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide food expiration tracking and reminders
  • Enable camera scanning and AI food recognition
  • Deliver voice notifications in your preferred language
  • Generate usage statistics and insights
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only: All your data is stored exclusively on your device using secure local storage mechanisms. We do not have access to your data, and it is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.
  • Data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption
  • No cloud storage or external data transmission
  • You have complete control over your data
  • Data can be deleted by uninstalling the app

4. Permissions We Request

4.1 Camera Permission

Used exclusively for scanning food items and detecting expiration dates. Photos are processed locally and not stored permanently unless you choose to save them.

4.2 Notification Permission

Used to send you timely reminders about food expiration dates. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

4.3 Microphone Permission (Optional)

Used only for voice notification features. This permission is optional and the app functions fully without it.

5. Third-Party Services

Flash Shot Freshness Guard operates independently and does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app is designed to function completely offline.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since all data is stored locally, parents have complete control over their children's data usage.

7. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app
  • Modify: You can edit or delete any food items or settings
  • Delete: You can clear all data or uninstall the app
  • Export: You can export your data for backup purposes

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
