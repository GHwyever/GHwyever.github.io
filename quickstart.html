DateKeep Quick Start Guide

One page to get productive in minutes

1. Install and Open

  • Install DateKeep and open the app.
  • Grant camera and notifications when prompted.

2. Add Your First Item

  • Tap “Capture Item”. Take a clear photo of the item.
  • Select the number of days or the date on the pop-up numeric keypad.
  • Confirm to save. The item appears in “Your Items”.

3. Smart Reminders

  • Go to Settings → Notifications to choose reminder timing (e.g., 1–7 days before expiry).
  • Enable sound or voice alerts if desired.

4. Process Items

  • Swipe an item card → “Mark as Used”.
  • Select “Before Expiry” or “After Expiry”. This updates achievements.

5. Achievements

  • Saved vs Expired: a simple distribution to visualize outcomes.
  • Saving Rate: percentage of saved items among saved + expired.
  • Timely Rate: used before expiry among used items.
  • Zero‑expiry streak: consecutive days without waste/late use.

6. Free Trial & Subscription

  • Free Trial: 3 days of premium access. After trial, the app switches to free mode.
  • Free Mode: keep your data; add up to 5 items. Upgrade for unlimited items and advanced features.
  • Open the Subscription page from the app or visit /subscription.
Tip: You can continue using the app after trial. Your data is preserved. Upgrade anytime to unlock full features.

7. Privacy

  • Local‑first storage. Cloud features available when signed in.
  • No sensitive keys are stored in plain text.
